🐐 Goat Runner

About the Game

Goat Runner is an exciting endless running game where you control a brave goat racing through the desert. Jump over fences, avoid obstacles, collect coins, and use special power-ups to achieve the highest score.

🎮 Features

  • 🐐 Smooth Goat Animation
  • 🌵 Beautiful Desert Environment
  • 🚧 Fence Obstacles
  • 🪙 Coin Collection
  • ⚡ Speed Boost Power-up
  • ⏳ Slow Motion Power-up
  • 🏆 Local High Score Leaderboard
  • 🎵 Sound Effects & Background Music
  • 📱 Mobile & Desktop Compatible
  • 🌐 Play Online in Your Browser

🎯 How to Play

• Press SPACE or ↑ to Jump.

• Avoid hitting fences and obstacles.

• Collect coins to increase your score.

• Grab Speed Boost and Slow Motion power-ups.

• Beat your highest score!
